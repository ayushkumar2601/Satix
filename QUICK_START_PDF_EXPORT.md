# Quick Start: PDF Export Feature

## How to Use

### Step 1: Get Your Trust Score
1. Sign up or log in to Satix
2. Upload your financial data (or let the system seed demo data)
3. Wait for score calculation to complete

### Step 2: Export from Score Page
1. After score calculation, you'll be on the `/score` page
2. Scroll down to see the "Export as PDF" button
3. Click the button
4. Your PDF will download automatically as: `Satix_Trust_Score_Report_YYYY-MM-DD.pdf`

### Step 3: Export from Dashboard (Alternative)
1. Navigate to `/dashboard`
2. Look at the "Trust Score Trend" card
3. Click the small download icon in the top-right corner
4. PDF downloads instantly

## Troubleshooting

**PDF won't download?**
- Check your browser's download settings
- Ensure pop-ups aren't blocked
- Try a different browser

**Button shows "Generating..." forever?**
- Refresh the page
- Check browser console for errors
- Ensure you have a valid trust score

**PDF looks wrong?**
- Ensure you're using a modern browser
- Update your browser to the latest version
- Try Chrome/Edge for best results
